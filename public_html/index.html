Lake District Bike Network proposal

CycleStreets.net Transport Hack Team

March 17, 2016

This site describes a plan to develop and provide services for a Lake District Bike Network (LDBN). Our research and analysis has identified a series of interventions in the area, including a bicycle hire network, that will reduce congestion and greatly enhance opportunities for outdoor tourism and leisure.

The main elements of the plan, and service we will provide if this is funded, are:

  • A network of cycle hire points that will build on the emerging cycle rental industry in the area and nationally, led by organisations such as BikePlus
  • Provision of digital tools to assist wayfinding and transport information to visitors
  • Tools for transport planners to help development and management of the network

This represent a comprehensive suite of measures to reduce barriers to cycling in the Lake District, main element of which is the bike hire network.
